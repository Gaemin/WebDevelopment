About Us

ColorProject is a linguistic institute that specializes in teaching tonal languages with its patented, effective and efficient education methods. Tonal languages are exceptionally difficult to learn because the pitch contour of a syllable affects the meaning of the word. Chinese, Punjabi and Vietnamese are some examples of tonal languages. People had used little tone marks to inscribe tones. Pinyin is the most widely known type of this. However, they all lack visibility. ColorProject has solved this problem by denoting the tones with colors. In mid-2015, we started our pilot program called Five Color Chinese, our first implementation the method in teaching Chinese. Our students have proved that it is easy, fast, and effective way to learn Chinese!



Our Projects
