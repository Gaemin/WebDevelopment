About Us

         ColorProject is a start-up that specializes in educating tonal languages with its patented, tech-based, effective, and unique methods. Tonal languages are exceptionally difficult to learn because different tones of a syllable have different meaning. Chinese, Punjabi and Vietnamese are some examples of tonal languages.

        ColorProject has solved this problem by denoting the tones with colors. In mid-2015, we started our pilot program called Five Color Chinese, our first implementation the method in teaching Chinese. Our students have proved that it is easy, fast, and effective way to learn Chinese!



Our Projects


        Five Color Chinese is our first pilot program where we integrated our tone-inscription method. Chinese (Mandarin) is the most widely spoken language in the world and it has five tones. Pronouncing a Chinese word with different tones influences the meaning of the word. Learning Chinese has been exceptionally difficult because of the tones.

        Using pictures, special storytelling device, mobile techniques, and our tone-inscription method through colors, learners can recognize the tone of each syllable instinctively and pronounce it correctly with ease. Using our effective linguistic tools, ColorProject could pack the three-month average beginner's course to a 20-hour program. So, join now! Start speaking in Chinese within a week!
